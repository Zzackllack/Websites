Aufgaben (Die unbestimmten Mengenangaben some und any):

There are ______ kids playing in the park.

I would like to have ______ chocolate, please.

Is there______ milk in the refrigerator?

She didn't find______ interesting books at the library yesterday.

Can you find______ common ground with your colleagues on this issue?

I'm not sure if there are______ exceptions to this rule; we should check the documentation.

It's important to have______ understanding of quantum mechanics if you want to grasp the concept fully.

Aufgaben (Die Mengenangaben much, many und lots of/a lot of):

I don't have______ time to finish the project today.

She bought______ apples at the grocery store.

There are______ people waiting for the bus at the station.

Can you give me______ information about the new software?

I´ve spent______ time searching for a good gaming PC.

The team spent______ hours preparing for the upcoming competition.

There aren´t______ trees in the park.

He has traveled to______ countries around the world.

Are there______ bottles of water in the refrigerator?
